TOURIST IN JEDDAH

Welcome to Jeddah, the vibrant jewel of Saudi Arabia's Red Sea coast, where tradition and modernity converge in a harmonious dance.

As you step into this cosmopolitan city, get ready to be captivated by the rich tapestry of history, culture, and innovation that defines Jeddah.
In 2023, make your way to the historic district of Al-Balad, where ancient coral houses and bustling souks transport you back in time.
Stroll through narrow alleys adorned with intricately carved wooden windows and experience the authentic charm of a bygone era.
For a taste of contemporary elegance, venture to the King Abdulaziz Square, where the iconic King Fahd Fountain gracefully dances against the backdrop of the Red Sea.
